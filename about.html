Welcome to Oh My Brush!

I started this blog in 2011, and thanks to it, I discovered the world of Korean cosmetics in 2012. Over the years, I’ve tested countless Korean beauty products, and you’ll find hundreds of product reviews on the blog. Everything I share is based on my personal experience and opinion. I’m not a chemist or dermatologist, this is simply my passion. The blog now welcomes thousands of returning and new visitors each month, and it’s clear that the K-beauty community in Hungary is steadily growing. In 2016, I received the Beauty Blogger of the Year award at Cosmopolitan’s Blogger Day event for my work on Oh My Brush.

I’m passionate about everything related to South Korea, such as K-dramas, K-pop, Korean food, and traveling in Korea. I visited South Korea in 2017 and 2019, and even planned our honeymoon there at my husband’s request. That’s how much I fell in love with the country and its culture. I’m very curious, so I enjoy traveling, reading, and learning new things, whether it’s about languages or skincare. Writing has always been close to my heart, and I kept this blog going without a break for over 10 years. My skin type is combination with an oily T-zone, and I’m prone to hormonal breakouts. It’s good to keep that in mind when reading my product reviews. If you have questions, feel free to ask in my Facebook group. Not only I, but thousands of other Hungarian K-beauty lovers are happy to help.
